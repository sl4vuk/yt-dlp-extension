# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want **YT Bookmark Cleaner** to be a welcoming and respectful project for everyone.

As contributors and maintainers, we pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being kind and respectful
- Giving constructive feedback
- Accepting different viewpoints
- Taking responsibility for mistakes
- Helping others learn and contribute

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats
- Insults, trolling, or personal attacks
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional community

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior that is harmful to the community.

---

## Reporting

If you need to report unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainer through GitHub or any private contact channel provided by the repository.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and respectfully.
